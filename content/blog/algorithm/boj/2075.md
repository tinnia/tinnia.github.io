###### 입력

```sh
5
12 7 9 15 5
13 8 11 19 6
21 10 26 31 16
48 14 28 35 25
52 20 32 41 49
```

###### 출력

```sh
35
```

<br />

##### <h5 style="color:#C587AE;">💻 풀이</h5>

```python
import heapq

N = int(input())
heap = list(map(int, input().split()))
heapq.heapify(heap)
Min = heap[0]
for _ in range(N - 1):
    for i in map(int, input().split()):
        if Min < i:
            heapq.heappush(heap, i)
            heapq.heappop(heap)
            Min = heap[0]
print(heap[0])
```
