###### 입력

```sh
6
10 20 10 30 20 50
```

###### 출력

```sh
4
```

<br />

##### <h5 style="color:#C587AE;">💻 풀이</h5>

```python
n = int(input())
arr = list(map(int, input().split()))
dp = [1] * n
for i in range(1, n):
    for j in range(i):
        if arr[i] > arr[j]:
            dp[i] = max(dp[i], dp[j]+1)
print(max(dp))
```
